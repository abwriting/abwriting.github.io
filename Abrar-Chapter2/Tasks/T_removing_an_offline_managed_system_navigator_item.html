    • Removing an Offline Managed System Navigator Item

      Use the Managed System Status workspace to clear offline managed systems from the Navigator view.

      • Procedure
        • What to do Next

Procedure

  1. Click the Enterprise Navigator item.
    The default workspace opens.
  2. Click Workspace gallery, scroll to Managed System Status, and click the workspace.
  3. Right-click an *OFFLINE row, then click Clear offline entry.
The offline entry is now cleared from the Navigator until the managed system comes online.
